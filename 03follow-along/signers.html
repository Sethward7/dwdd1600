The Signers of the Declaration of Independence

Famous Signers:

Thomas Jefferson
Primary author of the Declaration of Independence, third President of the United States, and a key advocate for democracy and individual rights.
John Adams
Second President of the United States, a leading advocate for independence, and a prominent lawyer and diplomat.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned polymath, inventor, and diplomat who played a crucial role in securing French support during the American Revolution.
John Hancock
President of the Second Continental Congress and the first signer of the Declaration of Independence, known for his large and bold signature.
